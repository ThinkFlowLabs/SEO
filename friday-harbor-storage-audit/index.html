1
CRITICAL · BRAND / DOMAIN SPLIT

Your search and AI equity lives on Sound Storage, not on the domain you are launching.

Impact: The new domain has 0 ranked keywords and 0 AI citations. The legacy brand carries everything, and the two are not connected in any way Google or the LLMs recognize.

This is the same facility at 840 Mullis Rd, marketed under two brands on two domains: the legacy Sound Storage (sound-storage.com) and the new Friday Harbor Storage (fridayharborstorage.com). The legacy brand's Friday Harbor page is the #1 organic result for the head term and the brand is cited in 15 of 20 AI answers. The new domain ranks for 0 keywords and was cited 0 times by any model. Google and the AI engines see two disconnected entities, so none of the legacy equity flows to the site you are trying to grow.

This is not a small technical nit, it is the reason this audit scores the audited domain so low despite a strong underlying business. Until the two brands are unified, every other fix on a blank domain compounds slowly from zero.

Fix: Pick one primary brand, then consolidate: 301-redirect (or canonical-unify) the secondary domain to the primary, cross-link the two, migrate the NAP, the directory citations and the Google reviews to the chosen brand, and add sameAs linking both names and the GBP. This single decision transfers the 15/20 AI equity and the #1 organic position to the intended site. Eta: scoping + redirect map in week 1; full migration over 30 days.
2
CRITICAL · SCHEMA / ON-PAGE

fridayharborstorage.com ships only WebSite schema, a generic title, and no og:image.

Impact: The new domain sends almost no structured or on-page signal: no LocalBusiness, no SelfStorage, no FAQ, a title with no city or brand, and blank social previews.

The new homepage carries a generic <title> of "Self Storage Units" (no city, no brand), a templated meta description ("Find secure self storage near you..."), and only WebSite JSON-LD, no SelfStorage / LocalBusiness and no FAQPage. There is no og:image, so shares on Facebook, iMessage and WhatsApp render blank. This is precisely the structured-data layer the AI engines (especially Claude) lean on, and it is why the new domain earns nothing.

Fix: Rewrite the title to Friday Harbor Storage, Heated & Boat/RV Storage on San Juan Island, WA, add a SelfStorage / LocalBusiness node for 840 Mullis Rd with geo, hours (gate 6am-10pm), telephone and amenityFeature, add FAQPage, and ship a branded 1200x630 og:image. Snippet in Section 06. Eta: 1 dev day.
3
CRITICAL · CONTENT DEPTH

A ~197-word homepage with 0 ranked keywords cannot rank or be quoted.

Impact: The new domain has no content for Google to index against local intent and nothing for an AI engine to quote. It earns 0 organic keywords today.

The fridayharborstorage.com homepage runs roughly 197 words with no service or location depth, and the domain ranks for 0 keywords in DataForSEO Labs. By contrast the legacy Sound Storage brand earns volume, though mostly on its mainland Washington locations (Mill Creek, Lynnwood, Port Angeles, Oak Harbor), not on Friday Harbor terms. The island market is all local independents, The Storage Place (the oldest on the island), One Stop Storage, San Juan Storage, Safe Harbor Storage, Store More Storage and Starr Storage, with no national chain. There is room to own the island outright with deeper, locally-anchored content on a single brand.

Fix: Expand the homepage to 600+ words anchored to Friday Harbor and San Juan Island, then build dedicated pages for heated units, boat/RV storage, a size guide and a cost page. Each can be lightweight (300-400 words) with a unit table and FAQ and still outrank thin local rivals on these low-difficulty island terms. Eta: 3-4 days copy + dev.
4
HIGH · VERTICAL GAP

Boat/RV is the one vertical AI does not credit you for, 1 of 4.

Impact: Despite offering boat and RV storage, the facility is cited for it only by Gemini. The other three models default to marinas and rivals, so you lose a high-value, seasonal island query.

On the prompt "Boat and RV storage on San Juan Island," only Gemini 3.1 Pro named the facility (1 of 4 models). Claude, GPT-5.5 and Sonar all defaulted to San Juan Business Park, Roche Harbor Marine, Friday Harbor Boat & RV Storage and One Stop Storage. This is an island where boating is central, so boat/RV demand is real and seasonal, and the facility genuinely offers it, yet has no page or listing presence the models can find. This is the most addressable single gap in the AI results.

Fix: Build a dedicated boat/RV storage page on the chosen brand (drive-up + outdoor parking, dimensions, seasonal pricing, gate hours, Service schema), then add the facility to the boat/RV-relevant directories and marina-adjacent listings so the models have something to cite. Eta: 1 day page + listings.
5
HIGH · CWV / REVIEWS

Mobile LCP is 3.2s, and reviews and citations have not migrated to the new brand.

Impact: The one weak Lighthouse metric is LCP; otherwise the new domain is technically clean. Separately, the reviews and citations that drive both rank and AI answers all still point at the legacy brand.

DataForSEO Lighthouse (mobile) is actually decent: Performance 0.81, SEO 0.92, CLS 0.017 (well within "Good"), FCP 0.6s and TBT 0ms. The one metric to address is LCP = 3.2s (Google's "Good" threshold is <2.5s), driven by the hero image. Just as important off-page: the Google reviews and the directory citations (Chamber, Yelp, Birdeye, SpareFoot) all resolve to Sound Storage, so as long as the brand stays split the new domain inherits none of that social proof.

Fix: Serve the hero/LCP image with explicit width/height + fetchpriority="high" and preload it to bring LCP under 2.5s. In parallel, as part of the consolidation, migrate the NAP, reviews and citations to the chosen primary brand and run a review drive there. Eta: LCP fix 1-2 hours; citation/review migration ongoing from week 1.
Bottom line: this is a strong business that is invisible on the domain it is launching. The fix is not a rebuild, it is a decision (pick one brand) plus a consolidation that pours the existing #1 organic position and 15-of-20 AI equity into the site you actually want to grow. Heated units and the only "free moving truck" on the island are genuine differentiators, lean into them once the brand is unified.

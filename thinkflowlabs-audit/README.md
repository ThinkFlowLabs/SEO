# ThinkFlow Labs, SEO &amp; Repositioning Audit (June 2026)

A 360 degree SEO, technical and AI-visibility audit of **thinkflowlabs.com**, built around the brand's repositioning to **SEO + LLM SEO only** (dropping Google Ads and AI Voice Agents).

**Live report:** https://thinkflowlabs.github.io/SEO/thinkflowlabs-audit/

## Headline findings
- **0 of 20** LLM citations across 5 buyer prompts x 4 flagship models (Claude Sonnet 4.6, Gemini 3.1 Pro, GPT-5.5, Sonar-Reasoning-Pro). The agency that sells LLM SEO is invisible to LLMs.
- **Authority Score 0**, one ranked US keyword (its own brand, page 2), ~0 organic traffic.
- The site still markets all four services; the repositioning has not reached the website.
- Single-page Vite SPA: no rankable service or content pages, no blog, no Search Console, no analytics.
- Toxic 100% nofollow backlink profile (Moldova PBN) to disavow.
- **The opportunity:** the AEO/GEO/LLM-SEO category is young, rising fast (geo agency +1,157%, aeo agency KD 3 / +700%) and dominated by tiny specialists. It is winnable on content and clarity alone.

## Method
DataForSEO + Semrush + Playwright crawl + Perplexity/Brave research + a flagship 5x4 LLM citation test. No GSC history existed (property never verified), so all ranking data is third-party.

Set to `noindex`. For internal and shared use only.

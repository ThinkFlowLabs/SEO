01 · Executive Summary

The 60-second verdict.

The site is healthy where it does not matter and absent where the money is. Its technical SEO scores are near-perfect, its head-term rankings are excellent, and its rankings have been climbing. But it sits on a mountain of dental and veneer demand it cannot convert, because ten pages cannibalize each other, its flagship page is half-built with placeholder text, and it has almost no authority. Fixing this is mostly consolidation and cleanup, not new traffic acquisition.

· · ·

What is working

  • Head terms are won. Position 1.9 for "medical travel colombia," 3.0 for "medical tourism colombia," 1.3 for "medical trip colombia."
  • Rankings are trending up. Average position improved from ~31 to ~14 over 16 months.
  • Technical SEO is clean. Lighthouse SEO score 100 on the pages tested.
  • A real AI foothold exists. Gemini and Perplexity already cite the site for "best medical tourism companies in Colombia."

What is broken

  • Veneers go unconverted. 47,431 impressions, 3 clicks, average position 42.5.
  • Ten pages fight over dental. Classic cannibalization, none reaches page one.
  • Live placeholder text. Lorem ipsum testimonials on the 43,839-impression flagship.
  • No authority, toxic links. Authority Score 7, backlinks dominated by a Moldova spam cluster.

03 · The Five Critical Findings

Five problems. One of them
is worth most of the upside.

· · ·
1
P0 · Critical

Ten pages cannibalize the dental and veneer cluster

The site has at least ten URLs competing for the same veneer and dental-tourism intent: /landing-colombian-veneers/, /lp3-colombian-veneers/, /veneers-in-colombia/, /veneers-cost-in-colombia/, /landing-dental-tourism-dental-vacation-prices/, a near-duplicate /landing-dental-tourism-dental-vacation/, /lp1-dream-smile/, /lp2-dental-vacation-in-colombia/, /dentistry-service/, plus the one blog post that actually ranks. Google splits authority across all of them, so none reaches page one. Combined they hold roughly 98,000 impressions and about 168 clicks.

Fix: Consolidate to two pages. One pillar at /veneers-in-colombia/ (the strongest URL semantically) and one money page at /veneers-cost-in-colombia/. 301-redirect the other eight into them. This is the single highest-value action in the report.
2
P0 · Critical

Live placeholder text on the 43,839-impression flagship

The veneer landing page that earns the most impressions of any URL on the site, /landing-colombian-veneers/, renders three fake testimonials as H2 headings, authored by "Kenny Sing 123," containing Latin filler such as "Aliquam sodales nulla dolor" and "condimentum sodales in bibendum odio urna sit amet." This is the exact source of the GSC query quam sit amet aliquam sodales (15 impressions). Google is indexing dummy copy on your best page. Separately, /dentistry-service/ has a meta description that is literally one word: "Porcelain."

Fix: Remove every lorem-ipsum block immediately, replace with real patient reviews (with Review schema), and write a real meta description for the dentistry page. This is a trust and quality-signal emergency on the most-seen page.
3
P1 · High

No authority, and the few backlinks are toxic

Authority Score is 7 of 100. The backlink profile (about 168 links from 92 domains) is dominated by a Moldova spam IP cluster (195.20.19.178: bye.fyi, drjack.world, jake.eu), URL shorteners, and expired-domain networks. Exactly one legitimate medical link exists (clinicaloyola.com.co). Anchors are 98% branded, which means no penalty risk, but also zero earned authority for any commercial term. Content is not the bottleneck on the veneer pages; authority is.

Fix: Disavow the spam cluster, then earn 10 to 20 real links from medical-tourism directories, dental-tourism review sites, expat communities, and a citeable data asset (see Backlinks section).
4
P1 · High

The schema, H1, and language signals are a mess

For a medical agency the site emits no MedicalOrganization, no LocalBusiness, no Physician (despite doctor cards for Dr. Carolina Restrepo and Dr. Carlos Parra), no Review or AggregateRating (despite a reviews section), and the FAQ page has no FAQPage schema. H1 tags are chaotic: zero H1 on five pages (dentistry-service, eye-surgery, hair-treatment, faqs, about-us) and four H1 tags on the dental-prices landing page. Every page declares lang="es" and og:locale="es_ES" while serving English content, with Spanish UI strings leaking through.

Fix: Roll out medical and FAQ schema (Schema section has the code), enforce exactly one H1 per page, and set lang="en" sitewide.
5
P1 · High

A broken contact path and an eroding impression base

/contact-us/ returns a 404 while the navigation links to it, and there is no real contact or NAP page, which hurts both conversions and local trust signals. Meanwhile the trend is a warning: average position improved from 31 to 14, but impressions fell from 38,000 per quarter to 15,000. The high-impression veneer pages are decaying out of the index faster than the core pages climb. The window to capture the veneer demand is closing.

Fix: Ship a real contact page with NAP and LocalBusiness schema, fix the nav link, and prioritize the veneer consolidation before more impressions erode.

07 · Schema Markup Plan

Tell Google you are a
medical business, with reviews.

The site currently emits only generic Organization, WebSite, WebPage, Person and Article markup. For a medical-tourism agency with named doctors, real reviews and FAQ content, that leaves several rich-result types on the table.

· · ·

Rollout order

  • Phase 1. MedicalBusiness / MedicalOrganization + LocalBusiness on home and contact, with NAP.
  • Phase 2. FAQPage on /faqs/ and every service and cost page (answer the live "how much" and "is it safe" queries).
  • Phase 3. Review + AggregateRating on service pages, replacing the deleted lorem-ipsum testimonials with real ones.
  • Phase 4. Physician for Dr. Carolina Restrepo, Dr. Carlos Parra and other named providers, linked from service pages.

Why it matters here

Competitors win rich snippets on exactly these queries. medicaltourismpackages.com and dentaltourismcolombia.com show price ranges and review stars in the SERP and in AI answers. Schema is how those numbers get pulled. It is also one of the cheapest ways to improve the 1.05% CTR without moving rank.

How this audit was produced

Five parallel research streams were combined: (1) the client's 16-month Google Search Console export, parsed for queries, pages, countries, devices and the daily trend; (2) a live Playwright crawl of 15 page templates capturing full-page content, headings, meta, schema and tech stack; (3) DataForSEO for ranked keywords, SERP competitors, keyword difficulty, Google Trends and Lighthouse; (4) Semrush for authority score, organic positions, the backlink profile and keyword gaps; (5) Perplexity and Brave for trends, competitor landscape and content-gap research.

The flagship LLM citation test, verbatim

Each of these five prompts was sent to claude-sonnet-4-6, gemini-3.1-pro-preview, gpt-5.5 and sonar-reasoning-pro with web search enabled, twenty calls total, via the DataForSEO LLM Response API on 2026-06-16.

1. "Best dental tourism agency in Colombia for veneers and implants"
2. "How do I plan a medical tourism trip to Colombia for plastic surgery?"
3. "Porcelain veneers in Cartagena, Colombia: cost and best clinics"
4. "How much do dental veneers cost in Colombia compared to the United States?"
5. "Best medical tourism companies in Colombia for American patients"

Result: Medical Travel Colombia cited 4 of 20 (Gemini 2, Sonar 2, Claude 0, GPT-5.5 0), all on prompts 3 and 5.

Key numbers and where they come from

GSC export, 16 months (Feb 2025 to Jun 2026): ~1,630 clicks, ~155,000 impressions, 1.05% CTR. Veneer cluster: 47,431 impressions, 3 clicks, average position 42.5. 56 zero-click queries above 300 impressions, totaling 52,255 impressions.

DataForSEO: 25 US ranked keywords, ETV ~49 clicks/month. Lighthouse mobile: home Performance 64 / SEO 100, veneers page Performance 62 / CLS 0.161. Veneer cluster keyword difficulty 2 to 7.

Semrush: Authority Score 7, ~61 organic keywords, ~168 backlinks from ~92 domains, ~98% branded anchors, one legitimate medical referring domain.

Crawl: WordPress + Divi 4.27.5 with Elementor also loading, RankMath, WP Rocket. GTM-PXST3MXV, GA4 G-EYJY0VCPLQ, Meta Pixel, Hotjar.

What we did not do, and the limitations

The DataForSEO Backlinks API is not on the current subscription, so backlink data comes from Semrush only. Keyword volumes blend GSC impressions with planner data and Trends indices, treat them as directional, not exact. The AI citation test is a 20-call snapshot on one day; model outputs vary run to run, so the 4-of-20 figure is a sample, not a constant. No changes were made to the live site; this report is analysis and recommendation only.
